Data Science 4200 Final Project Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to out Data Visualizations final project webpage! Our topic relates to the characteristics/beliefs that exist in voters and their connection to their political affiliation. This is important as it can help politicians better understand their constituents/know what gets people excited to vote. This is also important as we can provide visualizations to show connections between beliefs and certain traits.

About the Data

Our data is based on a pre-election poll asking a series of questions that span from age, race, religion, income, political party, and other general beliefs. For each question it ranges in the response as some questions have a 1-4 (1 being strongly agree/4 being strongly disagree), while other questions are a binary 1-2 (Yes/No). After dropping large null rows we have 5836 total rows. This dataset comes from a survey done by FiveThirtyEight.
